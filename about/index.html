The Philosophy

A toilet is furniture. We design it like one.

Most bathroom fixtures are chosen last and noticed least — a functional afterthought behind the vanity and the tile. Cetio was founded on the opposite belief: that the toilet is the fixture used daily, by everyone in the home, and deserves the same discipline applied to the marble underfoot and the hardware on the door.

That discipline starts underneath the glaze — in the trap way, the flush architecture, the seat hinge — and only then moves to the silhouette. Beauty, in our shop, is a byproduct of things done correctly.

From Mold to Home

Four stages, none of them skipped.

01

Mold & Cast

Vitreous china is cast in a single-piece mold, fusing bowl and skirt so there's no seam left to fail.

02

Glaze & Fire

The trap way is fully glazed inside and out, then kiln-fired for a non-porous, stain-resistant surface.

03

Flush Calibration

Every silhouette is tuned on the Kirin or siphonic platform until it clears the bowl in one controlled pull.

04

Certify & Test

CUPC® certification and ADA height compliance are verified before a single unit ships to a showroom.
